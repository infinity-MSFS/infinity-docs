# Infinity Launcher

The **Infinity Launcher** is a cross-platform (Windows and Linux-x64) freeware downloader and installer, fully compatible with both `MSFS` and `FS2024`. Lightweight, secure, and visually appealing, the launcher delivers a seamless experience tailored to the needs of flight simulation enthusiasts.
